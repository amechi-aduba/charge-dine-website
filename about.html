What to know about Charge & Dine Rewards

We connect EV drivers with restaurants that offer on-site charging stations, so every stop can power both your car and your next meal.

Our Mission

At Charge & Dine Rewards, our mission is to make sustainable travel effortless by rewarding EV drivers for choosing restaurants that invest in charging infrastructure.

Our Vision

We envision a network where charging and dining are seamlessly integrated, helping reduce range anxiety while supporting local restaurants across Massachusetts and beyond.

How We Operate

Using real-time app data, we model electricity costs, charger maintenance, and subscriber activity to keep the platform sustainable for restaurants and rewarding for drivers.
